We provide a wide range of dynamic display solutions for all fixed or mobile installations: indoor or outdoor advertising totems, transformation of static signs into LED signs, video advertising trailers with LED screens from 3.5 to 16m², as well as the sale of DIY LED screens .

 

Renting one of our LED advertising trailers is the right choice if you want to run a mobile advertising campaign.

Our trailers are well equipped and autonomous, they will be useful for:

  • Broadcast a promotional message
  • Showcase the best photos and videos of your event
  • Broadcast a live sporting event
  • Send a safety message 
  • Make signage

Equipped with a 3G modem, it is possible to change the content remotely during rental.
